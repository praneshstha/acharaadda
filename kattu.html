Our Story

Made like Aama used to make it

Aama Ko Achar was born from a simple desire — to share the authentic taste of Nepali home cooking with everyone. Every jar is prepared the same way it has been for generations: fresh ingredients, traditional spices, and a whole lot of patience.

We source our spices locally, grind them by hand when possible, and never use artificial preservatives. When you open a jar, you open a little piece of Nepal.
